Our Culinary Journey

Founded in 2010 by renowned chef Alexander Pokyto, our restaurant has been on a mission to redefine fine dining through innovative techniques and exceptional ingredients.

Every dish tells a story of passion, tradition, and artistic expression. We source only the finest ingredients from local farmers and global artisans to create memorable culinary experiences.

2010: The Beginning

Chef Pokyto opens the first location with a vision to blend traditional techniques with modern innovation.

2015: First Michelin Star

Our dedication to culinary excellence is recognized with our first Michelin star, marking a milestone in our journey.

2020: Global Expansion

We expand our unique dining experience to major cities worldwide, bringing our vision to global audiences.
